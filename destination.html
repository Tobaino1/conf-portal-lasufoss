Panel presentations in the LOC sessions will be in English. Visa Visa and Passport Requirements To enter Nigeria, you may need a Passport and/or a Visa, depending on the country you live in. Citizens of the ECOWAS are not required to obtain a visa in order to enter any member country of the ECOWAS Area for tourism or business For visits of less than 90 days, a passport valid for at least three months after the end of their stay is necessary for visitors.

Getting to and around Lagos Getting to Lagos Lagos is the most industrious city in Nigeria, situated at the south west of the country. Its International airport (Muritala) is at 15 minutes from the city centre and from all the main hotels. Major European Airlines, as well as some North America Airlines, operate direct daily flights to Lagos, from the most important European Capitals and North America. Getting around in Lisbon There is a wide inexpensive network in all towns and cities. From the Airport Once landed at Muritala Airport, a scheduled bus is operating regular transfers between the Airport and the city centre. Catching a taxi is very easy and from the airport to the venue will cost approximately #5,000 - #7,000. Uber & Taxify Uber and Taxify are well-functioning in Lagos. Download their various app to get around.
